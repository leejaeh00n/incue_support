Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the INCUE app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by INCUE Team (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Third-party AI services and data we share

Certain features (including interview practice, business conversation practice, entry sheet (ES) assistance, group discussion, and aptitude-related features) use artificial intelligence. When you use these features, information you enter or record may be sent from your device to servers operated by the Service Provider and then to the following third parties for processing:

  • OpenAI, LLC — Used for speech-to-text (transcription), conversational AI, feedback generation, text generation, and related processing, depending on the feature.
  • Google LLC — In some cases (for example, certain aptitude-related content generation), the Service Provider may use Google's Gemini API. If that service is unavailable, processing may fall back to OpenAI as described above.

Depending on the feature, categories of data that may be sent include: text you type; audio from the microphone; transcripts of audio; session context needed to generate feedback; and related technical metadata required to operate the service.

The Application shows an in-app notice before you use AI-powered features and asks for your agreement. Do not enter highly sensitive personal data (for example, national ID numbers, payment card or bank account numbers) in the Application.

For how these providers handle data they receive, see OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages
  • The time spent on the Application
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device

Microphone / Audio: The Application accesses the microphone to record your voice where features require audio (for example, interview practice and business conversation practice). Audio may be transmitted to the Service Provider's servers and to the third-party AI providers listed above to transcribe speech and generate feedback. We do not use audio data for advertising or cross-app targeting. Audio is not stored longer than necessary to provide the requested feature.

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

For a better experience, the Service Provider may process personally identifiable information you provide, including audio (processed to provide feedback) and device information, as described in this policy.
